Building sentences.

What does DT know?
-) How sentences are formed.

How does DT form sentences?
-) Create dictionary and assign basic properties objects.
	#) House: Big
	#) Fly: small
	#) Fly: fast
	#) ...

-) DT inferes sentences based on given words.
	#) Given: Fly
		+) Fly is small	
		+) Fly is fast 
		+) ...
	#) Given Fly, annoying
		+) Fly buzz loudly.
		+) ...

-) At first, simple sentence with only one clause should DT form.
	#) "The door opened."
	#) "Jill reads."

-) The words should be random at first (to test simple sentences), but should still have meaning (random sentence generator).

-) DT should form compund sentences.

-) DT should form complex sentences.
